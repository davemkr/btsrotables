Sime Industrial Brakes

Sime Industrial Brakes manufacture Hydraulic and Electromagnetic brakes which are used as service brakes and emergency brakes. They are operated by hydraulic power packs or electrical coils. The brakes use either solid or ventilated discs or drums.

The following list the characteristics of these brakes:
• Fail safe braking 
• Braking by Spring application
• Electromagnetic release
• Manual lining wear compensation
• Brake pads with wear indicators
• Automatic lining wear compensation
• Opening proving switches for inputs into a PLC
• Manual release lever or hydraulic release
• Mounting can be done on vertical axis disc

• Marine atmosphere protection
• Adjustment to Reduced torque
• Supply of solid and ventilated discs
• Discs & drums mounted on Flexible couplings
• Load regulated lowering
• Flameproof protection

Applications include

• Winch brakes 
• Wind turbine brakes 
• Container crane brakes 
• Boom Brakes 
• Conveyors 
• Marine winches

Svenborg Brakes

Svenborg brakes are manufactured in Denmark, conform to ISO9001 and meet the highest standards for safety and reliability required in large braking applications and multi-function service braking. These brakes require very little maintenance due to few moving parts, offer good torque control and stability, high heat capacity due to large pads, fast reaction time and compact size that allows for easy installation. These brakes are not sensitive to environmental conditions and have many options available for fail-safe controlled braking. Sintered metal linings are also available.

Svenborg brakes have distributors globally, including Bay Transmission Services in South Africa where they are used extensively on belt conveyors (as in SA & Australia). Other examples are mine winders (Canada), wind turbines (Israel), nuclear power plant cranes (Sweden), off-shore oil rigs (Norway and Brazil), elevators (Finland), cranes (Denmark and China), ski-lifts (France), ship unloaders (England) and excavators (the Netherlands)
